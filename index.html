Bio

Born in New York City on May 20, 1981 and then raised in Massachusetts, Rachel Platten is an Indie Pop artist who independently released two albums in 2003 and 2011. She went on to sign with Columbia Records and release her debut single "Fight Song" in 2015.

Rachel has taken the world by storm, topping countless radio charts worldwide and making it into the platinum Billboard Hot 100 with her hit "Stand By You". She has become an Emmy Award winning artist and has released multiple platinum selling albums.
